WELCOME

Setting clear rules for games at a freshers party ensures that everyone has a fair and enjoyable experience. Here are some general rules and instructions that you can communicate to the participants before each game:

  • Mingle and interact with others.
  • Have fun and encourage fellow participants.
  • Be respectful of everyone's performances.
  • Abide by the decisions of the game coordinator.
  • Maintain a friendly and sportsman like attitude.

Participants should encourage a positive and cooperative atmosphere. The goal is to create an enjoyable experience for everyone and make sure everyone feels included and respected.
